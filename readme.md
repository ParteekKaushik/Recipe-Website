# Gulab Jamun and Barfi Recipe Project

Welcome to the Gulab Jamun and Barfi Recipe Project! This project aims to provide easy-to-follow recipes for two classic Indian sweets: Gulab Jamun and Barfi. Whether you're a seasoned chef or a beginner in the kitchen, you'll find these recipes simple and delicious.

## Recipes

### Gulab Jamun

Gulab Jamun is a popular Indian dessert made from milk solids and soaked in a sweet syrup. Follow these steps to create soft and decadent Gulab Jamuns at home:

- **Ingredients:**
  - Ghee (clarified butter)
  - Bread
  - Sugar

- **Instructions:**
  1. Prepare the dough by combining milk powder, flour, and ghee.
  2. Shape the dough into balls and fry until golden brown.
  3. Prepare the sugar syrup with sugar, water, cardamom powder, saffron, and rose water.
  4. Soak the fried Gulab Jamuns in the warm sugar syrup for a few hours before serving.

### Barfi

Barfi is a traditional Indian sweet made from condensed milk and sugar. Follow these steps to make creamy and indulgent Barfi squares:

- **Ingredients:**
  - Milk powder
  - Condensed milk
  - Ghee (clarified butter)
  - Cardamom powder
  - Nuts (optional)

- **Instructions:**
  1. Heat ghee in a pan and add condensed milk.
  2. Mix in milk powder and cardamom powder until thickened.
  3. Spread the mixture in a tray, garnish with nuts if desired, and allow it to set.
  4. Cut the cooled mixture into squares and serve as a delightful treat.
